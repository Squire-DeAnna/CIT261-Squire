Site Plan


1.Site name
Fone Flava
2. Purpose statement,
This site is to provide a store for phone accessories like phone cases
and dust plug s for individuals who want to express themselves.
3. Target audience description
Individuals who are between 15-35 yrs old who are very expressive.
Hipsters, nerds and extroverts.

representing sub-groups of the larger audience,
